<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The low voltage isolating switch market consists of three primary types: single-column disconnectors, double-column disconnectors, and three-column disconnectors. Single-column disconnectors are simple switches that provide a gap between the power supply and the equipment, ensuring isolation. Double-column disconnectors offer additional isolation through two columns of insulating material, reducing the risk of electric shock and improving safety. Three-column disconnectors provide even higher levels of isolation and protection by utilizing three columns of insulating material. These different types of disconnectors cater to varying levels of isolation and safety needs in different applications.</p></p>
