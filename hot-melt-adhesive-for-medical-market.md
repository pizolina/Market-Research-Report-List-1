<p><p>Hot melt adhesive, commonly used in the medical industry, can be categorized into two types. One type is SBS hot melt adhesive, which stands for styrene-butadiene-styrene. This adhesive offers excellent bonding properties and is commonly used for medical applications such as bandages and surgical dressings. The other type is SIS hot melt adhesive, which stands for styrene-isoprene-styrene. SIS hot melt adhesive excels in adhesion to various materials and is frequently used in medical products like wound closure strips and medical tapes. Both types of hot melt adhesives play a crucial role in ensuring reliable bonding and quality medical devices.</p></p>
