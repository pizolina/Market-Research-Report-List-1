<p><strong>The Train Wheel Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Wheel Sensor</li><li>Double Wheel Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Train wheel sensors are used to monitor the speed and movement of train wheels. In the Single Wheel Sensor market, sensors are placed on each wheel, allowing for precise tracking of individual wheel behavior. This provides accurate data on wheel wear, temperature, and abnormal vibrations. On the other hand, the Double Wheel Sensor market involves the use of sensors positioned between two wheels. This configuration enables continuous monitoring of the distance between the two wheels, ensuring proper alignment and preventing derailments. Both types of wheel sensors play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of train operations.</p></p>
