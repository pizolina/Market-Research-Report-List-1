<p><p>SSP fertilizer, also known as single super phosphate fertilizer, is a type of phosphatic fertilizer that contains phosphate as the main nutrient. <p><p>The SSP (Single Super Phosphate) fertilizer market is categorized into two types: SSP Powder and SSP Granulated. On the other hand, SSP Granulated is a spherical form of SSP fertilizer that offers better handling and application properties. It is often preferred by farmers due to its convenient and uniform distribution during spreading operations. Both types of SSP fertilizers are valuable options for promoting crop growth and enhancing soil fertility.</p></p>
