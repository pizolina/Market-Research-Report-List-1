<p><p>The self-checkout system market is categorized into three types: stand-alone self-checkout systems, wall-mounted self-checkout systems, and countertop self-checkout systems. Stand-alone systems are freestanding units placed throughout a store, allowing customers to scan and pay for their items without cashier assistance. Wall-mounted systems are fixed to a wall, typically near store exits, and offer quick and easy payment options. Countertop systems are compact units placed on store countertops, offering an efficient self-checkout experience for limited space areas. These systems aim to streamline the shopping process and enhance customer convenience.</p></p>
