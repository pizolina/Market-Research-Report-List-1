<p><p>The Automotive Powertrain Cooling System market comprises various types such as ATOC (Air-To-Oil Cooling), ATF (Air-To-Fluid Cooling), ITOC (Internal-To-External Cooling), and Others. ATOC involves using air to cool the engine oil, while ATF involves cooling transmission fluid with air. ITOC is a system where heat is transferred from the engine's internal components to an external heat exchanger. The "Others" category includes additional cooling system types that do not fit into the aforementioned classifications. These different cooling systems help regulate and maintain optimal temperatures within the powertrain to enhance overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
