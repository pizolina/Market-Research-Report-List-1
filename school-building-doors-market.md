<p><p>The school building doors market encompasses different types of doors, including swing doors and tandem gates. Swing doors operate by swinging open or closed on hinges and are commonly found in classrooms and entryways. Tandem gates, on the other hand, consist of two panels that slide or fold to create an opening. These gates are often used in larger areas, such as gymnasiums or auditoriums, providing a wider entrance and an easier flow of people. Both swing doors and tandem gates serve the purpose of enabling access to different areas within a school building efficiently.</p></p>
