<p><p>Paper converting adhesives are used in the paper industry for various applications. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly, commonly used for their low VOC content and easy cleanup. Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents and provide strong, quick-drying bonds. Hot melt adhesives, in the form of solid sticks or pellets, are heated to a liquid state and then applied, creating an instant bond as they cool and solidify. These adhesive types offer different benefits and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the paper converting process.</p></p>
