<p><ul><li>Swept Source Optical Coherent Based Optical Biometers</li><li>Partial Coherence Interferometry (PCI) Based Optical Biometers</li><li>Optical Low Coherence Reflectometry (OLCR) Based Optical Biometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Next Generation Optical Biometry Devices market includes various types such as Swept Source Optical Coherent Based Optical Biometers, Partial Coherence Interferometry (PCI) Based Optical Biometers, and Optical Low Coherence Reflectometry (OLCR) Based Optical Biometers. Swept Source Optical Coherent Based Optical Biometers utilize a swept laser source to provide accurate measurements of ocular dimensions. Partial Coherence Interferometry (PCI) Based Optical Biometers use interference patterns to determine the eye's axial length and other parameters. Optical Low Coherence Reflectometry (OLCR) Based Optical Biometers employ low coherence light to measure ocular dimensions non-invasively.</p></p>
