<p><p>X-ray inspection systems technology refers to the use of X-rays to examine objects or materials for various purposes. The market for X-ray inspection systems can be categorized into two main types: film-based technique and digital imaging technique. In the film-based technique, a traditional photographic film is used to capture the X-ray images, which need to be developed before analysis. On the other hand, the digital imaging technique involves the use of electronic sensors to directly capture and display the X-ray images in real-time, eliminating the need for film processing.</p></p>
