<p><p>The nonstick coating market is classified into three main types: FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene), PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene), and Ceramic. FEP is a type of coating made from a combination of ethylene and propylene monomers, providing excellent nonstick properties. PTFE is a polymer coating that offers exceptional heat resistance and low friction. Ceramic coatings are made from inorganic materials and are known for their high durability and scratch resistance. These coatings are widely used in various industries for their nonstick properties, allowing easy release of food or materials from surfaces.</p></p>
