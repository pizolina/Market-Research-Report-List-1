<p><p>InP lasers, also known as indium phosphide lasers, are semiconductor lasers that utilize indium phosphide as the active medium. The market is driven by the increasing demand for high-speed data transmission and the growing adoption of InP lasers in communication networks. InP lasers are ideal for use in optical communication systems due to their high-speed capabilities and wavelength compatibility with fiber-optic networks.</p><p>Moreover, the healthcare sector is another major market for InP lasers, especially in medical imaging and surgical procedures. InP lasers offer high-power outputs and precise control, making them suitable for applications such as laser surgery and ophthalmology.</p><p>The market is witnessing various technological advancements, including the development of InP laser diodes with higher power outputs and improved performance.
