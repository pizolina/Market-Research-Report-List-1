<p><p>Industrial packaging films are versatile materials used for wrapping and protecting goods during storage and transportation. Shrink films are heat-sensitive and when exposed to heat, they shrink tightly to fit the shape of the product, providing a secure and tamper-evident packaging. Stretch films, on the other hand, are designed to stretch and cling to products, providing stability and protection against moisture and dust. Other types include barrier films, which offer excellent protection against gases and odors, and laminated films, which combine different properties to provide specific packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
