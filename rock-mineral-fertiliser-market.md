<p><p>Rock Mineral Fertilisers can be categorized into two main types: General Purpose and Special Purpose market. General Purpose rock mineral fertilisers are designed to provide a broad range of nutrients needed for the overall growth and development of plants. They are suitable for a wide variety of crops and help in improving soil fertility. On the other hand, Special Purpose rock mineral fertilisers are formulated to meet specific nutrient requirements of particular plants or soil types.
