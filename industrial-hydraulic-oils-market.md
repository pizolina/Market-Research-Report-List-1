<p><p>The industrial hydraulic oils market is segmented into two main types: synthetic oil and mineral oil. Synthetic oil is a man-made lubricant that is designed to offer superior performance and protection for hydraulic systems. It is generally more expensive than mineral oil but provides better thermal stability, oxidation resistance, and extended drain intervals. On the other hand, mineral oil is derived from natural crude oil and is more commonly used due to its lower cost. However, it may require more frequent maintenance and has limitations in extreme temperatures and high-pressure applications.</p></p>
