<p><ul><li>Induction Motor</li><li>Brushless Electric Motor</li><li>Switched Reluctance Motor</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passenger vehicle motor market encompasses various types of motors that propel vehicles. Induction motors use electromagnetic induction to generate motion, providing a reliable and cost-effective solution. Brushless electric motors, on the other hand, eliminate the need for brushes and commutators, ensuring a more efficient and low-maintenance operation. Switched reluctance motors rely on the principle of magnetic reluctance to turn electrical energy into mechanical motion. Lastly, the "Others" category includes alternative motor types like permanent magnet synchronous motors or hybrid motors, offering unique features and advantages to cater to different vehicle requirements.</p></p>
