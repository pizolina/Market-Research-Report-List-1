<p><p>Dry scroll pumps are widely used in the semiconductor market due to their reliable and efficient operation. There are two types of scroll pumps: static vortex and moving vortex. Static vortex pumps create a vacuum by using stationary components, while moving vortex pumps rely on rotating scroll elements to generate a vacuum. Both types offer advantages such as oil-free operation, reduced contamination risks, and enhanced durability, making them suitable for semiconductor manufacturing processes that require clean and reliable vacuum systems.</p></p>
