<p><p>The Asphalt Roofing System market comprises three main types: Built-Up Roofing (BUR), Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS), and Atactic Polypropylene (APP). BUR is a traditional roofing system with multiple layers of asphalt and reinforcing materials. SBS is a modified bitumen roofing system that contains rubber modifiers, making it more flexible and durable. APP is also a modified bitumen system, but it includes plastic modifiers instead, providing enhanced weatherability and long-lasting performance. These three market types cater to different requirements and preferences in terms of strength, flexibility, and weather resistance.</p></p>
