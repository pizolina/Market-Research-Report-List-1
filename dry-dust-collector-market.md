<p><strong>The Dry Dust Collector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inertial Separators</li><li>Electrostatic Precipitators</li><li>Fabric Filters</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dry dust collector market consists of various types of dust collectors used for separating and removing dust particles from industrial processes. Inertial separators use the principle of inertia to separate dust particles from the gas stream. Electrostatic precipitators employ an electric charge to attract and remove dust particles from the air. Fabric filters, also known as baghouses, use fabric bags to capture and filter out dust particles. Other types of dry dust collectors include cartridge collectors, cyclone separators, and wet scrubbers, which are used in specific applications.</p></p>
