<p><p>BOPET packaging films refer to a type of film used for packaging applications. These films are categorized based on their thickness. Films with a thickness below 15 μm are suitable for lightweight packaging needs, such as sachets and small pouches. Films with a thickness between 15-30 μm are commonly used for flexible packaging applications, including wrappers for snacks and confectionery items. Films with a thickness ranging from 30-50 μm are ideal for medium-weight packaging requirements, such as stand-up pouches and retort packaging. Lastly, films with a thickness above 50 μm are used for heavy-duty packaging purposes, such as industrial bags and heavy-duty sacks.</p></p>
