<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) Transformers enable the transmission of both power and data over Ethernet cables, eliminating the need for separate power cables. There are two main types of PoE Transformers: Flyback Type and Forward Type. </p><p>Flyback Type transformers offer compact size, high efficiency, and low cost. They provide galvanic isolation and can support PoE applications up to 30W or higher, making them suitable for various power levels.</p><p>Forward Type transformers also provide isolation but are designed for lower power levels, typically up to 13W. They offer high efficiency, low cost, and are commonly used in applications with limited power requirements.</p></p>
