Plate type denitration systems use stacked plates with media-filled channels to separate and absorb nitrates from water. Corrugated type denitration systems employ a corrugated media structure to enhance surface area for nitrate adsorption. These denitration market types offer various solutions for the removal of nitrates, catering to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
