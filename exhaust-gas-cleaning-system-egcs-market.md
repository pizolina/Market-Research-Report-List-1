<p><ul><li>Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System</li><li>Mixed Waste Gas Purification System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Exhaust Gas Cleaning System (EGCS) market consists of three main types: Open Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System, Closed Loop Exhaust Gas Purification System, and Mixed Waste Gas Purification System. </p><p>An Open Loop system uses seawater to remove pollutants from exhaust gases before they are discharged into the atmosphere. A Closed Loop system circulates a scrubbing solution within the system, removing pollutants and preventing seawater discharge. </p><p>A Mixed Waste Gas system combines both open and closed loop technologies to minimize both seawater usage and chemical consumption while effectively reducing emissions.</p></p>
