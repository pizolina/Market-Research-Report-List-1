<p><p>Controlled valves are used in various industries to regulate the flow of liquids or gases. Pneumatic controlled valves are operated by compressed air, providing fast and precise control. Electric controlled valves use electric signals to open or close the valve, offering enhanced automation capabilities. Hydraulic controlled valves use fluid pressure to regulate the valve's position. These valves find applications in industries like oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing. Each market type offers specific advantages and choosing the appropriate valve depends on factors such as the flow rate, pressure, and required level of control.</p></p>
