<p><p>The High Purity SiC Powder market consists of two main types: β-SiC and α-SiC. β-SiC refers to the cubic crystal structure of silicon carbide and is mainly used in electronic devices due to its high thermal conductivity and hardness. On the other hand, α-SiC refers to the hexagonal crystal structure of silicon carbide and is primarily used in abrasives and refractories due to its excellent mechanical properties and high thermal stability. These two types of high purity SiC powder cater to different industries and applications, offering unique benefits depending on their crystal structure.</p></p>
