<p><p>The industrial dust particle counting system market consists of two main types: Fixed Type and Portable Type. </p><p>Fixed Type systems are installed at specific locations within industrial facilities to continuously monitor the concentration of dust particles in the air. These systems provide a real-time assessment of air quality and help companies identify potential health and safety risks.</p><p>Portable Type systems, on the other hand, are designed to be used on the go. They are mobile and can be easily carried to different locations within a facility or even used for outdoor applications. These systems are particularly useful for conducting spot checks or assessing air quality in areas where a fixed system may not be present.</p></p>
