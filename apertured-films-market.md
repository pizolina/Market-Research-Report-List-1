<p><p>Apertured Films market types include Monolayer and Laminated films. Monolayer films are single-layered films with apertures or small openings for various applications such as personal care products, medical supplies, and industrial packaging. They provide breathability, fluid management, and barrier properties. Laminated films, on the other hand, consist of multiple layers fused together, offering enhanced strength, durability, and additional functionalities like moisture resistance and increased barrier properties. They are commonly used in food packaging, agricultural films, and building and construction industries for their improved performance and versatility.</p></p>
