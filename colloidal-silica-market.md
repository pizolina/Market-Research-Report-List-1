<p><p>Colloidal silica is a versatile material used in various industries. There are different types of colloidal silica available in the market. Alkaline colloidal silica has a higher pH level and finds applications in catalysts, detergents, and electroplating. Acidic colloidal silica, with a lower pH, is used in the manufacturing of paper, textiles, and ceramics. Modified colloidal silica is tailored for specific applications like coatings and adhesives. Ordinary colloidal silica is a general-purpose material used in a wide range of industries like construction, electronics, and automotive.</p></p>
