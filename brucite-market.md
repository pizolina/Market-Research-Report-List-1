<p><p>Brucite is a mineral that is commonly used in various industries. There are two types of brucite markets: phanerocrystalline magnesite and cryptocrystalline magnesite. Phanerocrystalline magnesite refers to brucite with large crystals that can be easily seen with the naked eye. Both types have their own applications and uses, such as in manufacturing refractory materials, flame retardants, and environmental remediation products.</p></p>
