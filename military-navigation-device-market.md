<p><p>The military navigation device market consists of two main types: GPS devices and GNSS devices. GPS (Global Positioning System) devices use a network of satellites to determine precise location, speed, and time information. They are widely used by military personnel for navigation, targeting, and communication purposes. On the other hand, GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) devices encompass GPS as well as other satellite systems, such as GLONASS and Galileo, to provide enhanced positioning accuracy and reliability. Both GPS and GNSS devices play crucial roles in military operations, ensuring effective navigation and coordination.</p></p>
