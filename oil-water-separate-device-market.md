<p><p>The Oil Water Separate Device (OWS) market can be categorized into three types: Above Ground OWS, Below Ground OWS, and Marine OWS. Above Ground OWS refers to systems that are installed above the ground and typically used in industrial settings to separate and remove oil from water before it is discharged. Below Ground OWS, on the other hand, are installed underground and commonly used in areas with limited space. They also separate and remove oil from water but are designed for underground installations. Marine OWS systems are specifically designed for use in marine environments, such as ships or offshore platforms, where oil-water separation is necessary for environmental protection.</p></p>
