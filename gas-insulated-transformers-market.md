<p><p>Gas-insulated transformers (GIT) are types of transformers that use SF6 gas as an insulating medium. There are two types of GITs: full-insulated transformers and semi-insulated transformers. Full-insulated transformers have the windings completely immersed in SF6 gas, providing excellent insulation and high efficiency. On the other hand, semi-insulated transformers have only part of the windings immersed in gas, offering a more economical solution. Both types offer advantages such as compact design, high reliability, and low maintenance requirements, making them suitable for various applications in power transmission and distribution systems.</p></p>
