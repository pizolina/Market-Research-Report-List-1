<p><p>Ultrasonic piezo positioners are devices used for precise positioning in various applications. Linear positioners enable linear movement along a single axis, offering high accuracy and stability. They are commonly used in industries such as semiconductor manufacturing and microscopy. On the other hand, rotary positioners facilitate rotational movement around a central axis, allowing for precise angular positioning. These are widely employed in optical alignment, robotics, and aerospace industries. Both types of positioners utilize ultrasonic piezo technology, which converts electrical signals into precise mechanical motion using piezoelectric elements.</p></p>
