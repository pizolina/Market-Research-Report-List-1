<p><p>Transcervical female sterilization market consists of two main types: tubal ligation and transcervical implants. Tubal ligation involves the surgical sealing or blocking of the fallopian tubes, preventing the egg from reaching the uterus for fertilization. On the other hand, transcervical implants are small devices inserted into the fallopian tubes to create a barrier, preventing the sperm from reaching the egg. Both methods are minimally invasive and provide long-term contraception options for women who have completed their desired family size.</p></p>
