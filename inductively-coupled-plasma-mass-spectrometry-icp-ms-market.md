<p><p>Inductively Coupled Plasma-Mass Spectrometry (ICP-MS) is an analytical technique used to determine the concentration of trace elements and isotopes in a wide range of samples. It is widely used in various industries, including environmental monitoring, pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and forensic sciences, for applications such as metal analysis, trace element analysis, and isotopic analysis.</p><p>The future outlook for the ICP-MS market is positive.
