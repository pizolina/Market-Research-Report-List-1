<p><p>The Granular Polysilicon market can be categorized into two types: the Silane Method and the Metallurgical Method. Silane Method involves the decomposition of silane gas into silicon and hydrogen by heating it in the presence of a catalyst. This method is primarily used in the production of high-purity polysilicon for solar panels. On the other hand, the Metallurgical Method uses metallurgical grade silicon as a raw material and involves a series of chemical reactions to convert it into granular polysilicon, which is then used in various industries such as electronics and chemical manufacturing. Both methods cater to different market demands and applications.</p></p>
