<p><p>The commercial range hoods market consists of various types of hoods used in commercial kitchens. Wall-mounted canopy hoods are installed against the wall and are ideal for limited space kitchens. Island canopy hoods are mounted on the ceiling and are suitable for kitchens with cooking islands. Proximity hoods are designed to capture smoke and grease near the cooking equipment. Eyebrow hoods are compact and mounted above individual cooking appliances. Other types of hoods include downdraft hoods, condensate hoods, and custom-designed hoods, which cater to specific needs and requirements of commercial kitchens.</p></p>
