<p><p>The pharmaceutical cleanroom air shower market comprises two types: single blow cleanroom air shower and double blow cleanroom air shower. In a single blow cleanroom air shower, air is blown from the top in one direction to remove contaminants. On the other hand, in a double blow cleanroom air shower, air is blown from the top and the side walls in two directions, providing a more thorough removal of contaminants. These types of air showers are crucial in maintaining the cleanliness and sterility of pharmaceutical production environments.</p></p>
