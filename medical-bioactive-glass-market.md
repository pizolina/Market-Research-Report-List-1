<p><p>Medical bioactive glass is a special type of glass that has the ability to bond with living tissues, making it highly suitable for medical applications. It is made of silica, calcium, sodium, and phosphorus, and when exposed to body fluids, it releases ions that can stimulate the formation of new bone tissue. This unique property of bioactive glass makes it widely used in various medical fields, such as orthopedics, dentistry, and tissue engineering.</p><p>The medical bioactive glass market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 7.9% during the forecast period.
