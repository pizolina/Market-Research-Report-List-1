<p><p>The Digital Receptacle Tester market consists of two main types: GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) Receptacle Testers and AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter) Receptacle Testers. GFCI Receptacle Testers are used to identify any faults in the electrical circuit that can lead to electric shocks. On the other hand, AFCI Receptacle Testers are designed to detect any potential fire hazards caused by arc faults. Both types of testers are crucial for ensuring electrical safety and preventing accidents, but they serve different purposes in identifying specific faults within the circuit.</p></p>
