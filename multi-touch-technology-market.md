<p><p>Multi-touch technology refers to the ability of a device to detect and respond to multiple simultaneous touch inputs. There are two main types of multi-touch technology markets: Opaque multi-touch technology and transparent multi-touch technology. Opaque multi-touch technology is commonly used in devices like smartphones and tablets, where the touch sensors are integrated into the display screens. On the other hand, transparent multi-touch technology is utilized in larger displays like interactive kiosks and touch tables, where the touch sensors are placed on top of the display surface, allowing for a more immersive touch experience.</p></p>
