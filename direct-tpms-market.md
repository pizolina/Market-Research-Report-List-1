<p><ul><li>High Line System</li><li>Low Line System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct TPMS, or Tire Pressure Monitoring System, is a technology that monitors the air pressure in vehicle tires. It is divided into two main market types: High Line System and Low Line System. The High Line System is typically found in luxury vehicles and uses individual tire sensors to provide real-time pressure readings for each tire. In contrast, the Low Line System is commonly used in non-luxury vehicles and utilizes the vehicle's existing ABS sensors to indirectly measure tire pressure. Both systems play a crucial role in improving driving safety by alerting drivers to any underinflated tires.</p></p>
