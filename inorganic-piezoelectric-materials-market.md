<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Crystal</li><li>Piezoelectric Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inorganic piezoelectric materials market comprises two main types: piezoelectric crystals and piezoelectric ceramics. Piezoelectric crystals are made of single crystals, such as quartz, and exhibit strong piezoelectric properties due to their ordered atomic structure. They are commonly used in applications requiring high sensitivity, such as sensors and actuators. On the other hand, piezoelectric ceramics are polycrystalline materials, offering a broader range of compositions, shapes, and sizes. They are widely utilized for their excellent durability and high electromechanical coupling coefficient in applications like ultrasound devices and transducers.</p></p>
