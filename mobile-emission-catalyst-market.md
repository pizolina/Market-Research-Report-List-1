<p><ul><li>Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC)</li><li>Catalyzed Soot Filters (CSF)</li><li>Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR)</li><li>Three-Way Conversion Catalyst (TWC)</li><li>Motorcycle Catalysts</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mobile emission catalyst market consists of various types of catalysts used to reduce harmful emissions from vehicles. Diesel Oxidation Catalysts (DOC) convert harmful compounds in diesel exhaust gases into less harmful substances. Catalyzed Soot Filters (CSF) trap and burn particulate matter from diesel engines. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) systems use a catalyst to convert harmful nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and water. Three-Way Conversion Catalysts (TWC) reduce harmful emissions from gasoline engines. Motorcycle Catalysts are specifically designed for motorcycles. The "Others" category includes alternative catalyst technologies and solutions.</p></p>
