<p><p>Hyper-Converged Infrastructure (HCI) is a type of infrastructure that combines computing, storage, and networking functions into a single system and manages them through a software-defined platform. The Hypervisor market involves the use of a virtualization layer that enables the creation and management of virtual machines on physical servers. On the other hand, the VSA (Virtual Storage Appliance) market provides software-based storage solutions that abstract physical storage resources into virtual storage pools, thus allowing for efficient storage management and scalability. Both markets contribute to the overall HCI landscape by enhancing resource utilization and simplifying IT management.</p></p>
