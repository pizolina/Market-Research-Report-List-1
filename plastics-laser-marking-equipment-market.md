<p><p>The plastic laser marking equipment market can be segmented into two types: CO2 and solid-state. CO2 laser marking equipment uses carbon dioxide as the laser medium, producing an infrared laser beam. This type is commonly used for marking non-metallic materials, including plastics. Solid-state laser marking equipment uses a solid material, such as a crystal, as the laser medium. It is known for its high precision and speed, making it suitable for marking a wide range of materials, including plastics. Both types contribute to the growth of the plastic laser marking equipment market.</p></p>
