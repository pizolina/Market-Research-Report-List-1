<p><p>The industrial indoor overhead cranes market is segmented into two main types: double girder cranes and single girder cranes. Double girder cranes are designed with two beams that support the hoist and trolley. They offer higher lifting capacities and are suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, single girder cranes have only one beam and are more cost-effective. They are generally used for lighter loads and in areas with limited headroom. Both types of cranes play a crucial role in industries for efficient material handling and increased productivity.</p></p>
