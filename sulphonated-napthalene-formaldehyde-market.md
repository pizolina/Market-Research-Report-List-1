<p><p>Sulphonated Napthalene Formaldehyde (SNF) is a chemical compound used in the construction industry as a water-reducing admixture. SNF is available in two market types, namely powder and solution. Powder SNF is in a solid state and is typically added directly to concrete mixtures during the batching process. On the other hand, solution SNF is a liquid form that is easily soluble in water. It is often used in precast concrete or spray applications where a liquid admixture is preferred. Both powder and solution SNF serve the purpose of increasing the workability and strength of concrete while reducing water content.</p></p>
