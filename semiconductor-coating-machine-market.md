<p><p>The Semiconductor Coating Machine market is segmented into three types: PVD Coating Equipment, CVD Coating Equipment, and Others. PVD Coating Equipment refers to Physical Vapor Deposition machines that apply a thin layer of material onto the semiconductor surface using a vaporized solid material. CVD Coating Equipment, on the other hand, stands for Chemical Vapor Deposition machines that use a chemical reaction to deposit a thin film onto the semiconductor surface. Lastly, the Others category encompasses any other types of coating machines used in the semiconductor industry that do not fall into the PVD or CVD categories.</p></p>
