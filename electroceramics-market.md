<p><p>Electroceramics market consists of various types such as insulation porcelain, ceramic capacitor, ferroelectric ceramics, semiconductor ceramic, and ion ceramic. Insulation porcelain is used to insulate electrical components due to its high dielectric strength. Ceramic capacitors store and release electrical energy, while ferroelectric ceramics exhibit spontaneous polarization, making them useful for actuators, capacitors, and sensors. Semiconductor ceramics possess unique electronic properties and are used in devices like diodes and transistors. Ion ceramics are known for their ionic conductivity and find applications in fuel cells, sensors, and batteries.</p></p>
