<p><p>The oil-water separation purifier market comprises different types such as the hydraulic type and the sealed type. The hydraulic type uses hydraulic power to separate oil and water by subjecting the mixture to high pressure, enabling efficient separation. On the other hand, the sealed type uses a sealed container where the oil and water are separated by gravity. Both types offer effective solutions for separating oil and water, but they differ in their mechanisms, with the hydraulic type utilizing pressure and the sealed type relying on gravity.</p></p>
