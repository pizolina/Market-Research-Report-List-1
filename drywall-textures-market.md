<p><p>The drywall textures market encompasses various types of finishes used on walls and ceilings. Knockdown texture involves spraying the surface with texture and then smoothing it down to create a flattened look. Orange peel texture resembles the uneven texture of an orange peel, achieved through spraying and drying techniques. Popcorn texture has a bumpy, granular appearance created by spraying a mix of texture and paint. Sand swirl texture incorporates swirling patterns achieved by applying joint compound in a circular motion. Lastly, "Others" refer to alternative textures not specifically mentioned above.</p></p>
