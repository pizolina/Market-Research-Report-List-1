<p><p>The two-cycle engine oils market can be divided into two types: air cooled oil and liquid cooled oil. These oils have unique properties to withstand higher temperatures and provide better lubrication in air-cooled engines. On the other hand, liquid cooled oil is formulated for engines that use a liquid coolant, such as cars and boats. These oils are designed to provide effective lubrication and cooling for engines operating at lower temperatures.</p></p>
