<p><p>In the in-flight broadband market, there are different types of technologies and frequency bands used to provide connectivity. L-band refers to lower frequencies that offer reliable global coverage but limited data capacity. Ku-band utilizes higher frequencies for increased data capacity but requires more satellites and has limited coverage in remote areas. HTS (High Throughput Satellites) market employs advanced technology to provide significantly higher data capacity, enabling faster and more reliable internet access for passengers. These different market types cater to various needs and coverage requirements in the in-flight broadband industry.</p></p>
