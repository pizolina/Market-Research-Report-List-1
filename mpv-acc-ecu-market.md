<p><p>The MPV ACC ECU market can be categorized into two types: OEM and Aftermarket. OEM refers to Original Equipment Manufacturer, where the ECU is produced and supplied by the vehicle manufacturer itself. On the other hand, the Aftermarket market comprises ECUs that are produced by third-party manufacturers and are not affiliated with the original vehicle manufacturer.
