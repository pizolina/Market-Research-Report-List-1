<p><p>Capos are devices used in music to change the pitch of a guitar's strings. Elastic capos stretch over the guitar neck, providing quick and easy adjustments. Premium capos are high-quality and durable, often made with premium materials. Toggle capos have a lever that is toggled to clamp the strings, allowing for one-handed use. Trigger capos use a spring-loaded clamp to hold the strings, providing more control over the tension. Other capos include partial capos for selective string manipulation or specialized capos for specific instruments like banjos or ukuleles.</p></p>
