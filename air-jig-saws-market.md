<p><p>The air jig saws market can be divided into two main types: corded jigsaws and cordless jigsaws. Corded jigsaws are powered by electricity and require a power cord to operate. They provide a continuous power supply and are suitable for heavy-duty tasks. On the other hand, cordless jigsaws are powered by rechargeable batteries, offering greater mobility and flexibility. They are ideal for working in areas without electricity or where maneuverability is essential. Both types have their advantages, and the choice depends on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
