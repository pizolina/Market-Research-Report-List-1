<p><strong>The Thermal Conductive Grease Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The thermal conductive grease market can be categorized into two types: silicone-based grease and non-silicone-based grease. Silicone-based grease is made from silicone oil and a thickening agent, offering good thermal conductivity and stability at high temperatures. It is commonly used in applications where high thermal conductivity is required, such as in electronics and automotive industries. Non-silicone-based grease, on the other hand, is made from a combination of organic and inorganic ingredients. It is preferred in applications where silicone-based grease cannot be used due to potential issues with silicone contamination.</p></p>
