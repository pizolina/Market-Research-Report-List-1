<p><p>Heart Rate Monitors (HRMs) are devices used to measure a person's heart rate during physical activities. The market for HRMs can be categorized into two types: chest-strap HRMs and wrist-based HRMs. Chest-strap HRMs consist of a sensor that is worn around the chest and a display unit worn on the wrist. They are known for their accuracy and are preferred by serious athletes. On the other hand, wrist-based HRMs have the sensors built directly into the watch-like device worn on the wrist. They are more convenient and comfortable for everyday use but may be less accurate during intense exercise.</p></p>
