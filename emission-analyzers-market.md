<p><p>Emission analyzers are devices used to measure and analyze the emissions of gases and particles in various industries and environmental applications. The market for emission analyzers can be categorized into different types. Non-dispersive infrared (NDIR) analyzers utilize infrared absorption to detect and quantify gases. Flame ionization detector (FID) measures organic compounds by burning the sample and detecting the ions produced. Chemiluminescence analyzers (CLA) measure concentrations of nitrogen oxides. Constant volume samplers (CVS) collect and analyze emissions in real-time. The market also includes other types of analyzers that utilize different technologies for emissions analysis.</p></p>
