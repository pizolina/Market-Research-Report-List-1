<p><p>Collaborative Pharmaceutical Robots are categorized into several market types based on their configuration and functionality. Articulated robots have jointed arms and can move in multiple directions, offering flexibility and precision. SCARA robots are typically used for assembly tasks and have a limited range of motion. Delta/Parallel robots are known for their high-speed and precision in pick-and-place operations. Cartesian robots move in a three-dimensional space and are suitable for handling heavy payloads. Finally, other market types encompass specialized robots with unique features and capabilities tailored for specific pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>
