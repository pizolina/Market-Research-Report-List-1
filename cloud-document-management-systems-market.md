<p><p>Cloud Document Management Systems (DMS) are categorized into three market types: Public Cloud, Private Cloud, and Hybrid Cloud. Public Cloud DMS offers document storage and management services through third-party providers, enabling easy accessibility, scalability, and cost savings, but with limited control and security. Private Cloud DMS, on the other hand, involves hosting the system on a private network, providing enhanced security and control but with higher costs. Hybrid Cloud DMS combines both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to benefit from the advantages of both models while addressing specific requirements and security concerns.</p></p>
