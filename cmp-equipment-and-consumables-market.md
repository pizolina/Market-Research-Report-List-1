<p><p>CMP stands for Chemical Mechanical Planarization, which is a critical process used in the semiconductor industry. Key trends in the market include the use of slurry-free CMP, which eliminates the need for abrasive slurries and reduces chemical consumption, and the adoption of advanced pad conditioning technologies for better control of process parameters.</p><p>The CMP equipment and consumables market is also experiencing consolidation, with major players acquiring smaller companies to expand their product portfolios and enhance their market presence.
