<p><strong>The PVDC Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Polymerized PVDC Film</li><li>Suspension Polymerized PVDC Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDC film, or polyvinylidene chloride film, can be classified into two main types based on the method of polymerization used in its production. Emulsion polymerized PVDC film is manufactured by dispersing PVDC particles in water with emulsifiers, resulting in a clear and glossy film suitable for food packaging due to its excellent barrier properties. On the other hand, suspension polymerized PVDC film is made by suspending PVDC particles in an organic solvent, and it is mainly used for industrial applications such as wire and cable insulation due to its high heat resistance and electrical insulation properties.</p></p>
