<p><p>Zinc plated mild steel refers to a type of steel that is coated with a layer of zinc to provide protection against corrosion. The zinc coating acts as a sacrificial layer that corrodes before the underlying steel, thereby extending its lifespan.</p><p>The market for zinc plated mild steel is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
