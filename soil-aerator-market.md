<p><strong>The Soil Aerator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil aerator market consists of two main types: mechanical and pneumatic. Mechanical soil aerators use rotating blades or tines to create small holes or slits in the soil, allowing for better air and water circulation. These aerators are typically larger and require more effort to operate. On the other hand, pneumatic soil aerators use compressed air to inject a burst of air into the soil, breaking up compacted layers and improving soil structure. Pneumatic aerators are generally smaller and easier to handle, making them suitable for smaller areas or residential use.</p></p>
