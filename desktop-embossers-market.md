<p><ul><li>Chip Encoding (Contact or Contactless)</li><li>Magnetic Stripe Encoding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Desktop embossers are devices used to create raised tactile characters on plastic cards. In terms of chip encoding, there are two types: contact and contactless. Contact chip encoding involves physically inserting a card into a reader, while contactless chip encoding allows for wireless communication. On the other hand, magnetic stripe encoding refers to the process of writing data onto a magnetic stripe on the card. These encoding methods are essential for card authentication and data storage purposes in various industries such as finance, transportation, and identification.</p></p>
