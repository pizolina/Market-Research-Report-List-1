<p><p>Oxygen bleaching stabilizers are chemical compounds that are used to enhance the performance and stability of oxygen-based bleaching agents. These stabilizers help to prevent the decomposition of hydrogen peroxide during the bleaching process, ensuring that the bleaching agents remain effective for a longer period of time. This helps to improve the overall efficiency of the bleaching process.</p><p>The oxygen bleaching stabilizer market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. There are several factors driving this growth, including the increasing demand for eco-friendly and sustainable bleaching agents in various industries such as textiles, pulp and paper, and household cleaning. Oxygen bleaching stabilizers are considered to be safe and environmentally friendly alternatives to chlorine-based bleaching agents, which are harmful to both human health and the environment. This has led to a growing adoption of oxygen bleaching stabilizers in various end-user industries.</p><p>Moreover, the growing consumer preference for cleaner and whiter clothes, coupled with the increasing focus on hygiene and cleanliness, is further fueling the demand for oxygen bleaching stabilizers in the textile and laundry industry.
