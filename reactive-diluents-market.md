<p><strong>The Reactive Diluents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Aliphatic</li><li>Aromatic</li><li>Cycloaliphatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Reactive diluents are used in various industries as additives to reduce the viscosity of certain materials, such as adhesives, coatings, and resins. There are three main types of reactive diluents: aliphatic, aromatic, and cycloaliphatic. Aliphatic diluents are derived from alkanes and offer excellent chemical resistance. Aromatic diluents, derived from aromatic hydrocarbons, provide high solvency and fast curing. Cycloaliphatic diluents, derived from cycloalkanes, exhibit excellent UV resistance and thermal stability. The selection of a specific reactive diluent type depends on the desired characteristics and end-use application of the material.</p></p>
