<p><p>The ETFE Tubing market consists of two main types: Heat Shrinkable Tubing and Extruded Tubing. Heat Shrinkable Tubing is designed to shrink in diameter when heated, providing a protective seal around objects. It is commonly used for insulation, wire harnessing, and cable bundling. On the other hand, Extruded Tubing is made by pushing molten ETFE material through a die, resulting in a continuous tube. This type of tubing is known for its high chemical resistance, excellent electrical properties, and durability, making it suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
