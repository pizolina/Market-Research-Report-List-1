<p><ul><li>Cell Analyzers</li><li>Cell Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow Cytometry Instruments are used for cell analysis and sorting. Cell Analyzers are instruments that analyze the characteristics of cells, such as size, shape, and protein expression. They provide detailed information about individual cells. On the other hand, Cell Sorters are instruments that not only analyze cells but also have the ability to physically separate or sort different types of cells based on their characteristics. They are useful for isolating specific cell populations for further analysis or experimentation. Both types of instruments are important tools in biomedical research and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
