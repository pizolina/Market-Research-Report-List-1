<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Pressure controllers for the semiconductor market are divided into three main types: absolute pressure controllers, gauge pressure controllers, and differential pressure controllers. </p><p>Absolute pressure controllers measure pressure relative to a vacuum, ensuring accurate readings regardless of atmospheric pressure changes. Gauge pressure controllers measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly used for general pressure control applications. Differential pressure controllers compare the pressure difference between two points, primarily used for flow and level control systems. These controllers play a crucial role in maintaining precise pressure levels during semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
