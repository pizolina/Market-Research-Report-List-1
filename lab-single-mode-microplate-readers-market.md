Fluorescence Plate Readers are designed to measure the fluorescent signal emitted by labeled molecules. Absorbance Plate Readers measure the absorption of light by a substance in a sample. Luminescence Plate Readers detect and analyze the light emitted from a sample due to a chemical reaction. These different types of plate readers cater to specific research or diagnostic needs in laboratories.</p></p>
