Serial scanners use a serial cable to connect with computers and are often used in industrial environments. USB scanners connect via USB ports and are widely used for their versatility and ease of installation. Wireless scanners offer freedom of movement, transmitting data to a computer or mobile device without the need for physical connection.</p></p>
