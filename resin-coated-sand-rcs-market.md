<p><p>Resin-coated Sand (RCS) is a type of sand that is coated with a layer of resin to improve its strength and performance in various applications. The market is driven by the increasing demand for high-quality castings in the automotive and construction industries. The growth of these industries and the growing emphasis on efficiency and quality in manufacturing processes are boosting the demand for resin-coated sand.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing adoption of hydraulic fracturing techniques in the oil and gas industry is also driving the demand for resin-coated sand. The sand is used as a proppant in hydraulic fracturing, where it helps to hold open the fractures created in the rock formation, allowing the extraction of oil and gas. The growing exploration and production activities in the oil and gas sector are expected to drive the demand for RCS further.</p><p>Furthermore, manufacturers are focusing on developing advanced resin-coated sand products with improved performance characteristics.
