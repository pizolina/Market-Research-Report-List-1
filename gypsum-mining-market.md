<p><p>Gypsum mining is the extraction of gypsum, a mineral that is widely used in various industries such as construction, agriculture, and healthcare. Gypsum is a naturally occurring mineral composed of calcium sulfate and water, often found in large deposits near the Earth's surface. The mining process involves the removal and crushing of gypsum rock, followed by the heating of the crushed material to remove any remaining water content, resulting in the formation of gypsum powder or plaster.</p><p>The future outlook for the gypsum mining market appears promising. Common gypsum deposits are the most abundant and widely available type, making them suitable for a range of applications.
