<p><strong>The Plant Extracting Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extracting Concentrators</li><li>Extracting Tank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Plant extracting equipment market includes two main types: extracting concentrators and extracting tanks. Extracting concentrators are devices used to extract and concentrate plant extracts by evaporating the solvent or carrier fluid. They utilize heating elements to evaporate the liquid, leaving behind a concentrated extract. On the other hand, extracting tanks are specialized containers used to hold the plant material and solvent mixture during the extraction process. These tanks are designed to optimize the extraction efficiency and maintain the desired temperature and pressure conditions for extracting the plant's active compounds.</p></p>
