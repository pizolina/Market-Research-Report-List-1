<p><p>The Air Automobile Horn market consists of two main types: Motor Pump Horn and Electric Air Horn. Motor Pump Horns operate by using a combination of an air compressor and a motor pump, which creates a loud sound. They are commonly used in heavy vehicles and require regular maintenance. On the other hand, Electric Air Horns are powered by electricity and produce a powerful sound. They are relatively easy to install and are often preferred in smaller or mid-sized vehicles. Both types are essential safety features that alert others on the road of a vehicle's presence.</p></p>
