<p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) resins are widely used in various industries due to their excellent thermal, mechanical, and chemical properties. The PPS market can be categorized into three types: linear PPS, cured PPS, and branched PPS. Linear PPS is a high-performance material with good dimensional stability and high strength. Cured PPS is created through a cross-linking process, resulting in enhanced heat resistance and chemical resistance. Branched PPS has a more complex molecular structure, providing improved melt flow, impact resistance, and processability. These different types cater to diverse application requirements in industries such as automotive, electrical, and aerospace.</p></p>
