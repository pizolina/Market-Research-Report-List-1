<p><p>A welding curtain is a protective cover used in welding operations to prevent sparks, debris, and harmful light from the welding process from entering the surrounding environment. It provides a barrier between the welder and the rest of the workspace, ensuring safety and preventing accidents. Welding curtains are typically made of fire-resistant materials such as PVC, vinyl, or fiberglass, and are often tinted to provide protection against harmful ultraviolet and infrared radiation.</p><p>The welding curtain market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years. The increasing emphasis on worker safety and the implementation of stringent occupational health and safety regulations are driving the demand for welding curtains across various industries, such as manufacturing, construction, oil and gas, and automotive. Additionally, the growth of the industrial sector, particularly in emerging economies, is further fueling market growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in welding curtain technology, such as the development of lightweight and portable curtains with enhanced fire-resistant properties, are also contributing to the market expansion.
