<p><p>Snowflake salt is available in two market types - granule and lamellate. Granule market refers to the form of snowflake salt that is processed into small, coarse grains. It is often used in cooking, baking, and seasoning due to its ease of use and quick dissolving properties. Lamellate market, on the other hand, refers to the flake-like structure of snowflake salt, which forms thin, flat crystals. Lamellate salt is commonly used for decorative purposes, garnishing dishes, and adding texture to various culinary creations.</p></p>
