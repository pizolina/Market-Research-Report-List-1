<p><p>PH/ORP sensors are used in various industries to measure the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. There are two main types of PH/ORP sensors: glass type sensors and ISFET sensors. Glass type sensors are made of a pH-sensitive glass membrane that generates a voltage based on the pH level of the solution. ISFET sensors, on the other hand, use ion-sensitive field-effect transistors to measure the change in voltage caused by the pH level. Both types of sensors are widely used and offer different advantages and applications depending on the specific needs of the industry.</p></p>
