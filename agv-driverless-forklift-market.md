<p><p>AGV Driverless Forklifts are available in different types based on their navigation systems. Laser Navigation Type utilizes laser sensors to map the surroundings and guide the forklift accurately. Magnetic Navigation Type relies on magnetic strips or tapes installed on the floor to create a route for the forklift to follow. Visual Navigation Type uses cameras and computer vision technology to recognize and navigate through the environment. Each type offers specific advantages and the choice depends on factors such as the complexity of the surroundings and the required precision.</p></p>
