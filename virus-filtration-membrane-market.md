<p><p>Virus filtration membrane market includes two types: ultrafiltration membrane and nanofiltration membrane. Ultrafiltration membrane is used to separate larger particles from a liquid, such as virus contaminants, by applying pressure. It has a pore size of 10-500 nanometers. On the other hand, nanofiltration membrane is more advanced and can separate even smaller particles and molecules due to its smaller pore size range of 1-10 nanometers. Both types of membranes play a crucial role in preventing the spread of viruses and ensuring the purity of liquids in various industries.</p></p>
