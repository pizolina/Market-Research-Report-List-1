<p><p>Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy Systems market includes two major types: Monoplace Chamber and Multiplace Chamber. Monoplace Chamber refers to a hyperbaric oxygen chamber that accommodates only one patient at a time. It is smaller in size and is considered suitable for individual treatments. On the other hand, a Multiplace Chamber is larger in size and allows multiple patients, as well as medical personnel, to undergo treatment simultaneously. It is designed for more complex treatments and can facilitate medical interventions if required. Both types serve the purpose of delivering high-pressure oxygen to patients for therapeutic benefits.</p></p>

<p><p>Hyperbaric oxygen therapy systems are used in various medical applications, including wound healing, decompression sickness, infection treatment, gas embolism, and others. In wound healing, the therapy helps increase the amount of oxygen delivered to the affected area, promoting tissue repair. Decompression sickness arises from the rapid change in pressure, and hyperbaric oxygen therapy reduces nitrogen bubbles in the body to alleviate symptoms. Infection treatment involves using high-pressure oxygen to improve the body's ability to fight infections. Gas embolism occurs when gas bubbles enter the bloodstream, and the therapy helps in the reabsorption of these bubbles. Other applications may include carbon monoxide poisoning, radiation injury, and traumatic brain injury.</p></p>
