<p><p>Dental facebows are an essential tool used in dentistry for the accurate restoration and alignment of teeth. There are two main types of dental facebows: Arcon Dental Facebows and Non-Arcon Dental Facebows. Arcon Dental Facebows have a hinge axis located in the condylar region, allowing for more precise and predictable measurements. Non-Arcon Dental Facebows, on the other hand, lack this feature and are generally less expensive. Both types serve the purpose of establishing a proper relationship between the jaws and dental articulator, ensuring accurate denture fabrication and occlusion.</p></p>
