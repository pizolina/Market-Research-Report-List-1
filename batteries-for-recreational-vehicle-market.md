<p><p>The recreational vehicle (RV) market offers three major types of batteries: lead-acid batteries, lithium-ion batteries, and absorbent glass mat (AGM) batteries. Lead-acid batteries are the traditional choice, known for their affordability but require regular maintenance. Lithium-ion batteries are more expensive but offer longer lifespan, high energy density, and lightweight design. AGM batteries are a type of lead-acid battery with enhanced performance, as they are spill-proof, require little maintenance, and have a longer lifespan compared to traditional lead-acid batteries. These battery options cater to different needs and preferences within the RV market.</p></p>
