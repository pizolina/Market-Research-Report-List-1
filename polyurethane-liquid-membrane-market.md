<p><p>Polyurethane Liquid Membrane is a market that can be categorized into two types: One Component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane and Two-component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane. The One Component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane is a single-component system that does not require any additional mixing before application. It is convenient to use and suitable for smaller projects. On the other hand, the Two-component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane is a two-part system that requires mixing of two components before application. It provides better versatility and is commonly used in larger-scale projects to ensure high performance and durability.</p></p>
