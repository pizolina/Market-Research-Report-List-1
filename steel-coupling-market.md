<p><p>Steel coupling market types can be classified into two categories: rigid coupling and flexible coupling. Rigid couplings are designed to provide a secure, non-moving connection between two shafts, ensuring precise alignment and efficient power transmission. On the other hand, flexible couplings are used to compensate for misalignment between shafts and absorb shocks and vibrations, allowing for smoother operation and increased durability. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, depending on the application requirements and system characteristics.</p></p>
