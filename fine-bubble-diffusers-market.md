<p><p>The Fine Bubble Diffusers Market consists of different types of diffusers, namely Disc Diffusers, Tube Diffusers, Plate Diffusers, and Dome Diffusers. Disc Diffusers are flat and circular in shape, providing high mixing and aeration efficiency. Tube Diffusers are long and narrow, mainly used in deep tanks to release air bubbles at the bottom. Plate Diffusers are rectangular and cost-effective, suitable for larger tanks. Dome Diffusers are spherical in shape and are used in wastewater treatment plants for their ability to evenly distribute air bubbles.</p></p>
