<p><p>EVC charging stations can be categorized into two main types: on-board charger and off-board charger. An on-board charger is built directly into the electric vehicle, allowing for convenient charging through a standard power outlet. On the other hand, an off-board charger is a standalone unit that is separate from the electric vehicle, usually found at charging stations or homes. Off-board chargers generally provide faster charging speeds and higher power levels. Both types play a crucial role in expanding the electric vehicle charging infrastructure and enabling widespread adoption of electric vehicles.</p></p>
