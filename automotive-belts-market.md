<p><p>Automotive belts are essential components used in vehicles to transfer power and control various engine parts. Drive belts, also known as serpentine belts, are responsible for driving multiple engine components such as the alternator, power steering pump, and air conditioning compressor. They are typically made of rubber and are commonly used in modern vehicles. Timing belts, on the other hand, synchronize the rotation of the crankshaft and camshaft, controlling the opening and closing of valves. They are crucial for the engine's optimal performance and are usually made of rubber with high-tensile cords for durability.</p></p>
