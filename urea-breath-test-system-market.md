<p><ul><li>Scintillation Technology</li><li>Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Urea Breath Test System market consists of two types of technologies: Scintillation Technology and Isotope Ratio Mass Spectrometry (IRMS). Scintillation Technology uses a radioactive substance that emits particles when it comes into contact with urea in the breath, allowing for easy detection and measurement. On the other hand, IRMS relies on the measurement of isotopic ratios to determine the presence of specific substances in the breath, providing a high level of accuracy and sensitivity. Both technologies are used for diagnosing various gastrointestinal disorders and detecting the presence of Helicobacter pylori bacteria.</p></p>
