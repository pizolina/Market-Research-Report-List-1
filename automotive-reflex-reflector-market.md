Smart reflex reflectors are equipped with sensors and communication capabilities, allowing them to provide real-time information and interact with other vehicles or infrastructure. The integration of reflex reflectors in ADAS aims to improve road safety by enabling vehicle-to-vehicle communication and enhancing the vehicle's awareness of its surroundings.</p></p>
