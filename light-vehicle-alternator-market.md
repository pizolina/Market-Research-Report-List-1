<p><ul><li>Salient Pole Type</li><li>Smooth Cylindrical Type</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The light vehicle alternator market consists of two main types: Salient Pole Type and Smooth Cylindrical Type. Salient Pole Type alternators have protruding magnetic poles, which provide a higher flux density and increased power density. These alternators are commonly used in high-power applications, such as heavy-duty trucks and buses. On the other hand, Smooth Cylindrical Type alternators have a compact design with a smooth cylindrical shape and inbuilt rectifiers. They are generally used in smaller vehicles, such as passenger cars and motorcycles, due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
