<p><p>The automotive inflator market can be broadly categorized into two types: portable and fixed. Portable inflators are compact and lightweight devices that can be easily carried and used on the go. These inflators are typically powered by a rechargeable battery or 12-volt power outlet in vehicles. On the other hand, fixed inflators are permanently installed within a vehicle or at a specific location, such as a gas station or repair shop. These inflators are usually more powerful and can handle higher pressure requirements. Both types cater to the needs of different consumer segments and offer convenience and safety in inflating automotive tires and other inflatable objects.</p></p>
