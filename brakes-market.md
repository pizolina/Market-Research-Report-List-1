<p><p>The brake market consists of various types of components that are essential for the braking system of vehicles. Brake pads are friction materials attached to the brake caliper, which press against the brake rotor to generate braking force. Brake shoes are similar to brake pads but are used in drum brakes. Brake lining is the friction material used in heavy-duty applications such as trucks and buses. Brake rotors are the flat disc-like components that the brake pads press on to generate friction. Brake drums are round, hollow components used in drum brakes to generate braking force.</p></p>
