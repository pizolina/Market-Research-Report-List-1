<p><p>Nickel powder for plating is available in two main types: carbonyl nickel powder and electrolytic nickel powder. Carbonyl nickel powder, also known as nickel carbonyl, is produced through a thermal decomposition process of nickel tetracarbonyl. It is primarily used for high-performance applications such as automotive parts manufacturing and electronics. Electrolytic nickel powder, on the other hand, is produced through an electrolysis process and finds applications in electroplating, corrosion resistance, and catalysts. Both types serve a critical role in the plating industry and have distinct characteristics and market demand.</p></p>
