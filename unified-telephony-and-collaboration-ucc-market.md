UCC market is categorized into three types: on-premise, cloud, and hybrid. On-premise UCC involves installing and managing the system on-site, offering high control and security but requiring significant investment. Cloud UCC is hosted remotely, providing flexibility, scalability, and cost savings but relying on internet connectivity. Hybrid UCC combines both on-premise and cloud solutions, offering a balance between control and convenience.</p></p>
