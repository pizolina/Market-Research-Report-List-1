<p><p>The adaptive solar collectors market is divided into several types, including flat plate collectors, evacuated tube collectors, solar air collectors, and others. Flat plate collectors are the most commonly used type, consisting of an insulated box with a black absorber plate to capture sunlight. Evacuated tube collectors consist of rows of glass tubes that contain a heat absorbing fin. Solar air collectors use air as the heat transfer medium, achieving heating through solar radiation. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various innovative and less common types of adaptive solar collectors.</p></p>
