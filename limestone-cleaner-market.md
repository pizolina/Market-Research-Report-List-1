Limestone cleaners are formulated to effectively dissolve and remove these impurities without damaging the delicate surface of the stone.</p><p>The global limestone cleaner market has been growing steadily in recent years, and this growth is expected to continue during the forecast period. The market is driven by various factors including the increasing awareness among consumers about the importance of regular maintenance and cleaning of limestone surfaces.
