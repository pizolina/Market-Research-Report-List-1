<p><p>COVID-19 test kits are available in three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT), Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA), and Neutralization Assay. RDTs provide quick results in minutes, detecting viral proteins or antibodies. ELISA uses an enzyme-based reaction to detect antibodies, requiring more time and specialized equipment. Neutralization assay measures the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus and is conducted in a laboratory setting. Each type of test kit offers different advantages and is used based on the requirements of healthcare professionals.</p></p>
