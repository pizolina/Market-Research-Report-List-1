<p><p>Pediatric influenza treatment market consists of various types such as antiviral drugs, antipyretics, neuraminidase inhibitors, and others. Antiviral drugs are medications that specifically target the influenza virus to suppress its growth and replication in the body. Antipyretics are drugs that help reduce fever, a common symptom of influenza, by lowering the body temperature. Neuraminidase inhibitors inhibit the activity of neuraminidase enzymes, which are essential for the virus to spread and cause infection. "Others" category includes additional treatments and interventions used for pediatric flu management such as respiratory support and fluids.</p></p>
