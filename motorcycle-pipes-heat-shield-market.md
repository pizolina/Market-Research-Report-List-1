<p><p>The motorcycle pipes heat shield market includes two main types: header heat shields and exhaust pipe heat shields. Header heat shields are designed to protect the headers (the pipes that connect the engine to the exhaust pipe) from excessive heat, preventing any damage to nearby components. On the other hand, exhaust pipe heat shields are built to shield the exhaust pipes from high temperatures, ensuring the rider's safety by reducing the risk of heat-related injuries. Both types of heat shields are crucial in maintaining the motorcycle's performance and preventing any potential harm.</p></p>
