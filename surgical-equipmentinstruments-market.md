<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Surgical Equipment/Instruments market?</strong></p>
<p><p>Some emerging trends in the global surgical equipment/instruments market include the increased adoption of minimally invasive procedures, advancements in robotic surgery technologies, and the rising demand for disposable surgical instruments. Minimally invasive procedures are preferred by patients and surgeons due to shorter recovery times and lesser post-operative complications. Robotic surgery technologies offer enhanced precision, control, and visualization for surgeons, leading to improved surgical outcomes. The demand for disposable surgical instruments is growing due to their convenience, reduced risk of infections, and cost-effectiveness. Additionally, there is a shift towards the use of energy-based surgical instruments, which provide faster healing and reduced blood loss during surgeries.</p></p>
