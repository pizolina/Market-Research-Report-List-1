<p><p>Footwear adhesives are used for bonding various components of shoes together. Solvent based footwear adhesives are popular due to their strong bonding ability, but they release harmful volatile organic compounds. Water based adhesives offer similar bonding strength without the environmental concerns. Radiation cured footwear adhesives use ultraviolet or electron beams for quick curing, resulting in high productivity. Hot melt footwear adhesives, on the other hand, provide instant and strong bond as they solidify upon cooling. Each type of adhesive has its own advantages and is used depending on specific footwear manufacturing requirements.</p></p>
