<p><p>The center stack panel display market consists of two major types: TFT LCD and OLED. TFT LCD, or Thin-Film Transistor Liquid Crystal Display, utilizes a backlit panel that generates images by manipulating liquid crystals. It offers good image quality, cost-effectiveness, and is widely used in automotive infotainment systems. On the other hand, OLED, or Organic Light-Emitting Diode, employs organic compounds to emit light when an electric current is applied. This technology provides high contrast ratios, wide viewing angles, and deeper blacks, making it suitable for premium automotive displays. Both TFT LCD and OLED serve as essential components in enhancing user experiences in vehicles.</p></p>
