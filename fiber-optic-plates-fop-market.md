<p><p>Fiber Optic Plates (FOP) are used to distribute light uniformly over a large area. Straight FOPs are flat plates that maintain the same thickness throughout, while tapered FOPs have a gradually decreasing thickness. Straight FOPs are suitable for applications with limited space or where uniform illumination is required. Tapered FOPs are used when a specific light distribution or focusing is needed, as the taper helps in shaping the light output. Both types of FOPs play a crucial role in transmitting and managing light in various industries.</p></p>
