<p><p>The Liquid Applied Waterproofing Membrane System market is classified into two types: NCM Type and NCA Type. NCM Type refers to "Non-Crosslinked Membrane," which includes systems that do not form a crosslinked structure upon application. This type provides good water resistance and flexibility. NCA Type, on the other hand, stands for "Nonsolvent Based Acrylic," which is a membrane system that utilizes acrylic as its main component and doesn't require solvents. NCA Type systems offer excellent adhesion and durability, making them ideal for waterproofing applications.</p></p>
