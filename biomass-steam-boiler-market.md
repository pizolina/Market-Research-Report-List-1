<p><p>The Biomass Steam Boiler Market includes two main types: Combined Heat & Power (CHP) Systems and Cogeneration. CHP systems utilize biomass in generating electricity and heat simultaneously, offering energy efficiency and cost savings. Cogeneration refers to the process of producing electricity and reusing the generated heat for other purposes within a specific facility or building. Both CHP systems and cogeneration help maximize the potential of biomass combustion, reducing the dependency on fossil fuels for energy production and promoting sustainable and eco-friendly practices in the market.</p></p>
