The market for these machines can be classified into three types based on their level of automation. </p><p>Manual machines require human intervention for each step of the packaging process. They are suitable for small-scale operations with low production rates and limited budgets.</p><p>Semi-automatic machines have partially automated functions that assist with carton feeding, film wrapping, and heat sealing processes. They are versatile and suitable for medium-sized businesses with moderate production volumes.</p><p>Automatic machines are fully automated and can perform all packaging tasks independently. They are ideal for large-scale operations with high production rates, optimizing efficiency and reducing human labor.</p></p>
