<p><p>The Activated Alumina market is comprised of two main types, namely Powdered Form Activated Alumina and Sphered Form Activated Alumina. Powdered form refers to finely ground alumina particles, which are widely used for various applications such as adsorption, catalysts, and desiccants due to their high surface area. On the other hand, Sphered form consists of alumina particles that are spherical in shape, offering enhanced mechanical strength and attrition resistance. This form is commonly used in processes requiring high efficiency and low pressure drop.</p></p>
