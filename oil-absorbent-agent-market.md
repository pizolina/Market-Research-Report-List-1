<p><p>The oil absorbent agent market is categorized into three types: natural organic, natural inorganic, and synthetics. Natural organic absorbents are made from plant-based materials such as corn, coconut husks, or peat moss. Natural inorganic absorbents are derived from minerals like volcanic ash or clay. Synthetics are man-made absorbents, usually derived from polymers or chemicals. These absorbents are designed to effectively soak up and contain oil spills or leaks in industrial settings, environmental cleanup efforts, or automotive maintenance, ensuring proper disposal and preventing contamination.</p></p>
