<p><p>An Electrostatic Fabric Integrated Precipitator (EFIP) is an advanced air pollution control device that combines the principles of both electrostatic precipitation and fabric filtration. It is primarily used to remove particulate matter and aerosols from industrial exhaust gases, making it an indispensable tool in industries such as power generation, cement, chemical, and steel.</p><p>EFIPs work by charging particles in the exhaust gas stream using electricity and then collecting them on a fabric filter. This two-step process ensures highly efficient and effective removal of even the finest particles from the gas stream, leading to cleaner emissions and improved air quality.</p><p>The Electrostatic Fabric Integrated Precipitator Market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period.
