<p><p>The Heat Shrink Joint market consists of two main types - Core Heat Shrink Joint and 3 Core Heat Shrink Joint. In a Core Heat Shrink Joint, a single insulated cable core is connected to another cable core using heat shrink technology. This type of joint is commonly used for single core cable connections. On the other hand, a 3 Core Heat Shrink Joint allows the connection of multiple cable cores simultaneously using heat shrink technology. This type of joint is typically used for three-core cable connections. Both types ensure efficient and reliable electrical connections.</p></p>
