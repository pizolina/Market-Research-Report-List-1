<p><ul><li>Vertical Gas-Nitriding Vacuum Furnaces</li><li>Horizontal Gas-Nitriding Vacuum Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gas-nitriding vacuum furnaces are used for surface hardening of steel parts. There are two types: vertical and horizontal gas-nitriding vacuum furnaces. Vertical furnaces have a vertically oriented chamber, allowing for efficient loading and unloading of parts. They are suitable for processing long or bulky parts. Horizontal furnaces have a horizontally oriented chamber, making them ideal for processing smaller parts in large quantities. Both types provide a vacuum environment that protects the parts from oxidation during the nitriding process, resulting in improved wear resistance and increased lifespan.</p></p>
