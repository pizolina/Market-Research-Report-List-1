<p><strong>The Automotive Dyno Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Engine Dynamometer</li><li>Chassis Dynamometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive dyno market consists of two main types: Engine Dynamometer and Chassis Dynamometer. Engine Dynamometer is used to measure and analyze the performance of an engine by applying load and recording various parameters. It helps optimize engine functions and assess its power output. Conversely, Chassis Dynamometer measures the performance of a vehicle's drivetrain by simulating real-world driving conditions. It provides valuable data on power, torque, and emissions, making it useful for vehicle testing, development, and certification purposes. Both types play crucial roles in the automotive industry for enhancing engine and vehicle performance.</p></p>
