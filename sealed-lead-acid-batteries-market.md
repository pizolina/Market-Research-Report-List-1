<p><p>The Sealed Lead-Acid Batteries market is segmented into various types, including Absorbent Glass Mat (AGM) Batteries, Gel Batteries, and Other market. AGM batteries use a glass mat separator to hold the electrolyte, allowing for a higher power output and better resistance to vibration. Gel batteries use a gel-like electrolyte that immobilizes the sulfuric acid, making them spill-proof and resistant to high temperatures. The Other market encompasses other types of sealed lead-acid batteries, such as deep cycle and maintenance-free batteries, which have specific applications and features tailored to different industries.</p></p>
