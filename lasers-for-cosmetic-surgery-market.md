<p><p>The lasers for cosmetic surgery market can be divided into two main types: ablative laser and non-ablative laser. Ablative lasers work by removing the outer layers of the skin, stimulating collagen production and reducing the appearance of wrinkles, scars, and pigmentation. Non-ablative lasers, on the other hand, target the underlying layers of the skin without removing the surface, promoting collagen growth and improving skin texture and tone. Both types offer different approaches to address various cosmetic concerns, ultimately aiming to enhance the appearance and achieve desired results.</p></p>
