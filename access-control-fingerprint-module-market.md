<p><p>The Access Control Fingerprint Module market includes two main types: Capacitive Fingerprint Module and Optical Fingerprint Module. Capacitive modules use the electrical properties of the human body to detect and authenticate fingerprints. They offer high accuracy and are resistant to external factors like dirt or moisture. On the other hand, Optical modules capture an image of the fingerprint using light and sensors. They are reliable and cost-effective. Both types provide secure access control by ensuring only authorized individuals can gain entry based on their unique fingerprints.</p></p>
