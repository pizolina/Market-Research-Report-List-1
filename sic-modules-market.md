<p><ul><li>SiC MOSFET Type</li><li>SiC SBDs + SiC MOSFETs Type</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SiC Modules refer to power electronic devices that utilize silicon carbide (SiC) semiconductors. They are available in different types, including SiC MOSFET Type, SiC SBDs + SiC MOSFETs Type, and other market variations. SiC MOSFET modules utilize SiC metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistors, delivering high efficiency and lower power losses. SiC SBDs + SiC MOSFETs modules combine SiC Schottky barrier diodes (SBDs) and SiC MOSFETs, offering enhanced power handling capabilities. The "other" market segment encompasses SiC module types that may have diverse characteristics or combinations of SiC devices, catering to specific application requirements.</p></p>
