<p><strong>The Automotive Chassis Dynamometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Roller</li><li>Multi Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive chassis dynamometers are testing devices used to evaluate the performance characteristics of vehicles. Single roller dynamometers have only one rotating roller on which the vehicle's wheels are placed and tested. On the other hand, multi-roller dynamometers have multiple rollers that allow each wheel to be individually tested. Single roller dynamometers are typically more compact and cost-effective, suitable for lower vehicle weight classes. Multi-roller dynamometers provide more accurate and realistic testing, making them ideal for heavier vehicles and advanced performance analysis.</p></p>
