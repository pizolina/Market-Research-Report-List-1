<p><p>Pocket ventilation systems are commonly used in industries for drying paper and other materials. These systems are categorized into four types based on their functionality. Steam Heated Cylinders involve the use of steam to heat the cylinders and remove moisture from the materials. Multi-Cylinder Dryers consist of multiple drying cylinders that work simultaneously. Single-Tier Dryers have only one cylinder for the drying process. Flakt Dryers use a combination of hot air and mechanical means to dry the materials efficiently. These variations cater to different industrial requirements and provide effective drying solutions.</p></p>
