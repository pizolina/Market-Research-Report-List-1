<p><p>The wood-based flooring panel market consists of various types, including particleboard (PB), medium density fibreboard (MDF), high density fibreboard (HDF), oriented strand board (OSB), and plywood. Particleboard is made from wood chips and resin, MDF from fine wood fibers and resin, and HDF from highly compressed wood fibers. OSB is composed of strands of wood bonded together, while plywood is constructed by layering thin sheets of wood veneer. These different types offer varying levels of strength, durability, and affordability, catering to different consumer preferences and market demands.</p></p>
