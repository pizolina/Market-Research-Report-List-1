<p><p>De-oiled lecithin is a natural emulsifier derived from soybeans that is widely used in various industries. In the market, there are two common types of de-oiled lecithin: powdered and granulated. Powdered de-oiled lecithin is a fine, dry form that easily disperses in dry mixtures, making it ideal for applications like powdered beverages, baking mixes, and instant products. On the other hand, granulated de-oiled lecithin is coarser and more free-flowing, making it suitable for applications such as animal feed, chocolates, and confectionery products. Both types offer similar functionality as natural emulsifiers, but their varying physical properties cater to different product requirements.</p></p>
