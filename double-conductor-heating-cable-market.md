<p><ul><li>Self-regulating Heating Cable</li><li>Constant Wattage Heating Cable</li><li>Mineral Insulated Heating Cable</li><li>Skin-Effect Heat-Tracing Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The double conductor heating cable market consists of four types of heating cables: self-regulating heating cable, constant wattage heating cable, mineral insulated heating cable, and skin-effect heat-tracing cable. </p><p>Self-regulating heating cable adjusts its output based on the surrounding temperature, making it energy-efficient. Constant wattage heating cable provides a consistent heat output regardless of temperature changes. Mineral insulated heating cable is highly durable and resistant to high temperatures. Skin-effect heat-tracing cable is used for long-distance heat tracing applications, where an electric current flows through the outer conductor of the cable, heating the inner pipe or vessel.</p></p>
