<p><ul><li>Electric Stretcher</li><li>Hydraulic Stretcher</li><li>Manual Transfer Stretcher</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transfer stretcher market consists of different types of stretchers used for patient transfer. Electric stretchers are powered by electricity and enable easy lifting and adjusting of the stretcher. Hydraulic stretchers use hydraulic power to lift and adjust the stretcher, providing a smooth and controlled movement. Manual transfer stretchers rely on manual force for lifting and adjusting the stretcher. These stretchers are more cost-effective but require more physical effort. Each type has its advantages and suitability depending on the specific healthcare setting and patient needs.</p></p>
