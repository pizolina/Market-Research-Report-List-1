<p><p>The Paclitaxel Coated PTCA Balloon Catheter is a medical device used for the treatment of coronary artery diseases. It is a catheter with a balloon at its tip that is coated with a drug called Paclitaxel. When inserted into the narrowed coronary artery, the balloon is inflated to open up the blockage, while the drug coating releases Paclitaxel to prevent restenosis (re-narrowing) of the artery.</p><p>The market analysis of the Paclitaxel Coated PTCA Balloon Catheter indicates a positive outlook for the industry.
