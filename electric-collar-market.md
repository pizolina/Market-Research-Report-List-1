<p><p>Electric collars are categorized into two main types: tracking collars and training collars. Tracking collars are designed to help pet owners locate their animals when they wander off or get lost. These collars are equipped with GPS technology and allow owners to track their pets in real-time through the use of mobile apps or handheld devices. On the other hand, training collars are used for behavior modification and obedience training. They deliver a mild electric stimulation to the dog when they misbehave or fail to follow commands, helping them learn and respond to commands more effectively.</p></p>
