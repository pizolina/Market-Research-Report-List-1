<p><ul><li>Coil Spring</li><li>Rubber Metal Spring</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The train locomotive suspension market is divided into three main types: coil spring, rubber metal spring, and others. Coil spring suspension systems use helical springs made of steel to support the locomotive and absorb shocks. Rubber metal spring suspensions combine rubber elements with metal plates to provide flexibility and shock absorption. The "others" category includes alternative suspension systems such as air suspension or hydraulic suspension. These suspension types are used in train locomotives to enhance stability, reduce vibrations, and improve overall ride comfort.</p></p>
