<p><strong>The Medical C Arm System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Mobile C-arm</li><li>FPD Mobile C-arm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical C Arm System market includes two types of systems: CCD Mobile C-arm and FPD Mobile C-arm. CCD Mobile C-arms use Charge-Coupled Device technology for imaging, which captures X-ray images on a sensor. They are portable and commonly used for surgeries and diagnostic procedures. On the other hand, FPD Mobile C-arms use Flat Panel Detectors, which provide higher image quality and lower radiation dose. These systems are also portable and widely utilized in various medical settings for real-time imaging during surgeries and interventions.</p></p>
