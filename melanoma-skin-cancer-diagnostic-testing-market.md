<p><p>Melanoma skin cancer diagnostic testing involves the use of various techniques and tools to detect the presence of melanoma, a type of skin cancer that arises from the pigment-producing cells called melanocytes. Biopsy involves the removal of a small sample of tissue for microscopic examination. Molecular testing employs genetic and molecular markers to analyze the tumor tissue, assisting in the identification of genetic mutations and predicting the effectiveness of targeted therapies.</p><p>The future outlook for the melanoma skin cancer diagnostic testing market is promising.
