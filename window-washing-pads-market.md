<p><p>The window washing pads market can be categorized into two types: soft pads and hard pads. They provide a scratch-free cleaning experience and are suitable for use on delicate surfaces. On the other hand, hard pads are made from stiffer materials like nylon or plastic, and are ideal for removing tough stains or dirt from windows.
