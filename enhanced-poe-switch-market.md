<p><strong>The Enhanced PoE Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.3af PoE</li><li>802.3at PoE +</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Enhanced PoE Switch market comprises two main types: 802.3af PoE and 802.3at PoE+. The 802.3af PoE standard delivers up to 15.4 watts of power per port, enabling devices like IP cameras and VoIP phones to be powered through the Ethernet cable. On the other hand, the 802.3at PoE+ standard offers a higher power capacity of up to 30 watts per port, making it suitable for power-hungry devices such as wireless access points and pan-tilt-zoom cameras. These standards have revolutionized network installations by simplifying power delivery and reducing the need for additional power outlets.</p></p>
