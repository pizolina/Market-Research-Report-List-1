<p><p>Thermal inkjet print-heads are used in various markets, including continuous inkjet and random inkjet printing. Continuous inkjet is a type of printing where ink droplets are continuously generated and selectively charged to form the desired image. This method is commonly used in high-speed industrial printing applications. On the other hand, random inkjet printing involves individually depositing ink droplets to create arbitrary patterns or designs. This technology finds applications in areas like personalized printing, textiles, and packaging, providing versatile and customizable printing solutions.</p></p>
