<p><p>The commercial vehicle gasoline engine exhaust valve market consists of three types: Mono Metallic Valve, Bimetallic Valve, and Hollow Valve. Mono Metallic Valve refers to a type of valve made entirely from a single metal, providing good durability and resistance to high temperatures. Bimetallic Valve, on the other hand, is made by combining two different metals, resulting in improved heat resistance and reduced wear. Finally, Hollow Valve includes a hollow design, reducing weight and improving the overall performance of the engine exhaust valve.</p></p>
