Trailed rock pickers are towed behind a tractor and are ideal for larger fields. Mounted rock pickers are attached to the tractor's 3-point hitch, allowing for greater maneuverability and versatility. Semi-mounted rock pickers are a combination of trailed and mounted models, allowing for easy attachment and detachment. Each market type offers varying benefits, allowing farmers to choose the most suitable option based on their specific needs.</p></p>
