<p><p>The apparel reflective tape market is divided into two main types: glass beads materials and microprismatic materials. Glass beads materials are made up of thousands of tiny glass beads that reflect light back to its source, making the tape more visible in low-light conditions. Microprismatic materials, on the other hand, use tiny prisms to bounce light in multiple directions, increasing visibility from all angles. Both types of materials enhance the safety of apparel by improving visibility in various environments and lighting conditions.</p></p>
