This has led to the development of advanced brake systems, including anti-lock braking systems (ABS) and electronic brake force distribution (EBD) systems, which require reliable brake hoses and lines to function effectively.</p><p>Another trend observed in the automotive brake hoses and lines market is the increasing adoption of lightweight materials. Manufacturers are continuously exploring lightweight alternatives to conventional rubber hoses and steel lines to improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions.
