<p><p>Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE) materials encompass various types, including Styrenic Block Copolymers (TPS or TPE-S), Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV), and others. TPS or TPE-S refers to a class of TPE materials composed of styrene-based polymers, which provide excellent elasticity, flexibility, and low-temperature resistance. TPV, on the other hand, is a blend of rubber and thermoplastic material that combines the favorable characteristics of both, offering high durability, heat resistance, and good compression set properties. The "Other" category in the TPE market represents additional TPE variants like Polyurethane (TPU) and Thermoplastic Olefins (TPO), each with its unique properties and applications.</p></p>
