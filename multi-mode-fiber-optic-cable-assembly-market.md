<p><p>The multi-mode fiber optic cable assembly market is classified into various types based on the connectors used. ST connectors are the most common ones, providing a push-and-twist connection. FC connectors offer a screw-type interface for enhanced stability. LC connectors are smaller, allowing higher port density. MT-RJ connectors combine both fibers into a single connection. SC connectors are widely used, providing simple insertion and removal. Lastly, there are other connectors like MTP/MPO, SMA, etc., catering to specific requirements of different applications. These connectors enable reliable and efficient connectivity in multi-mode fiber optic cable assemblies.</p></p>
