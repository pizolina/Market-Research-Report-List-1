<p><strong>The Polyurethane Non-Woven fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spunbond non-woven fabric</li><li>Spunlace non-woven fabric</li><li>Needle punch non-woven fabric</li><li>Meltblown non-woven fabric</li><li>Wet laid non-woven fabric</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane non-woven fabric is a versatile material used in various industries. Spunbond non-woven fabric is created by extruding molten polymer through spinnerets, resulting in a strong and durable fabric. Spunlace non-woven fabric involves a hydroentanglement process where high-pressure water jets entangle the fibers, creating a soft and absorbent fabric. Needle punch non-woven fabric is made by mechanically interlocking fibers with barbed needles, achieving a dense and resilient fabric. Meltblown non-woven fabric is produced by extruding molten polymers onto a conveyor belt, forming a fine and filter-like fabric. Wet laid non-woven fabric involves a water-based bonding process, yielding fabrics with high strength and absorbency. Lastly, there are other markets for polyurethane non-woven fabric that entail custom manufacturing processes or specialized applications.</p></p>
