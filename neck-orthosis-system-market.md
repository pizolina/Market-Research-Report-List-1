<p><p>The Neck Orthosis System market can be divided into two main types: Inlay Type and Externally Assisted. Inlay Type neck orthosis systems are designed to provide support and stability to the neck by fitting snugly around it. They work by immobilizing the neck and limiting its motion. On the other hand, Externally Assisted neck orthosis systems use external devices to provide support and assistance to the neck. They are usually equipped with mechanisms such as straps and pulleys that help in adjusting and controlling the neck's movement.</p></p>
