<p><p>The infectious disease testing device market is comprised of various types, including molecular diagnostic tests, point-of-care tests (POCT), and immunodiagnostic tests. Molecular diagnostic tests utilize techniques like polymerase chain reaction (PCR) to detect and identify genetic material of infectious agents. POCT refers to tests that can be performed at the patient's location, providing rapid results. Immunodiagnostic tests rely on the detection of specific antibodies or antigens in bodily fluids to indicate the presence of an infectious disease. These testing devices play a crucial role in accurately diagnosing and managing infectious diseases.</p></p>
