<p><ul><li>RMR Analysis</li><li>VO2 Max Analysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metabolic Rate Analysis Software market includes two main types: RMR Analysis and VO2 Max Analysis. RMR Analysis focuses on measuring Resting Metabolic Rate, which determines the number of calories burned at rest. This data is useful for weight management and calorie intake planning. On the other hand, VO2 Max Analysis measures the maximum amount of oxygen a person can utilize during intense exercise and indicates their aerobic fitness level. This information is crucial for athletes and individuals aiming to improve their endurance and performance.</p></p>
