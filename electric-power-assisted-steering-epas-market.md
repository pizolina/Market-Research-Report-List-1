<p><strong>The Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-EPS</li><li>P-EPS</li><li>R-EPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Assisted Steering (EPAS) market consists of various types such as C-EPS, P-EPS, and R-EPS. C-EPS (Column-Electric Power Steering) utilizes an electric motor mounted on the steering column to assist in steering, resulting in improved maneuverability. P-EPS (Pinion-Electric Power Steering) utilizes an electric motor mounted directly on the steering rack, offering precise control and enhanced responsiveness. R-EPS (Rack-Electric Power Steering) employs an electric motor to assist in steering, integrated within the steering rack, ensuring smooth operation and reduced energy consumption. These EPAS types provide drivers with efficient and comfortable steering experiences.</p></p>
