<p><p>Rubber moulding machinery is classified into two main types: semi-automatic and automatic. Semi-automatic rubber moulding machinery requires some manual intervention during the moulding process, such as loading and unloading the rubber material. On the other hand, automatic rubber moulding machinery operates without much human intervention, as it is equipped with advanced technology and has automated features to handle the entire moulding process. Both types of machinery are utilized in the rubber moulding industry but vary in terms of efficiency and level of human involvement.</p></p>
