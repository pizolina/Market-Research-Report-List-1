<p><p>The Ortho and Osteobiologics market consists of different types of products used in orthopedic surgeries for bone grafting. Synthetic bone grafts are made from artificial materials that mimic the properties of natural bone. Allografts are taken from a donor and processed before being used in the patient. Blocks and strips are shaped pieces of bone grafts used for specific surgical needs. Other types of grafts include xenografts (animal-derived) and cell-based grafts. These products provide surgeons with options for promoting bone healing and regeneration in patients.</p></p>
