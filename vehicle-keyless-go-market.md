<p><p>Vehicle Keyless Go is a system that allows drivers to unlock, start, and lock their cars without using a physical key. There are two types of Keyless Go markets: Passive Keyless Access (PKES) and Remote Keyless Access (RKES). In PKES, the system automatically detects the presence of the key fob and unlocks the car when the driver approaches. RKES, on the other hand, requires the driver to press a button on the key fob to unlock and start the vehicle remotely. Both types provide convenience and enhance vehicle security.</p></p>
